How Our Program Works

The Complete Package

Our reconstruction program is designed as an integrated solution to ensure quality, efficiency, and maximum support for Ukrainian families:

Step 1: Application & Contract

Homeowners apply to our program and, upon approval, sign a formal reconstruction contract with Dreams Realized Charity and our partner organizations.

Step 2: Design Phase

The Dream-Building Architects & Entrepreneurs Volunteer Association creates a custom architectural design for your home at no cost. Our volunteer architects work with you to ensure the design meets your family's needs.

Step 3: Financial Support

Upon contract signing, Dreams Realized Charity provides $5,000 USD or equivalent value in home essentials including appliances, textiles, and kitchen equipment. Military families receive additional furniture support.

Step 4: Construction

Sunwell Group manages the complete construction process using high-quality materials and professional crews. Your home will be completed within 90-120 days.

Step 5: Move-In & Beyond

After completion, families move into their new homes and can optionally participate in our tourism homestay program to generate additional income.

Why a Package Deal?
By working with all three organizations together, we ensure quality control, streamlined processes, and maximum efficiency in rebuilding Ukraine. This integrated approach allows us to provide comprehensive support while maintaining high standards and reasonable timelines.

News & Updates

$524 Billion Reconstruction Need

As of February 2025, Ukraine requires approximately $524 billion over the next decade to repair and rebuild infrastructure, housing, and essential services. The housing sector alone accounts for $84 billion of reconstruction needs, with 13% of Ukraine's housing stock damaged or destroyed.

Housing Crisis Response

Over 2.5 million Ukrainian households have been affected by damaged or destroyed housing. International organizations, including the World Bank, UN, and European Commission, are coordinating massive reconstruction efforts focusing on priority areas including housing, energy, transport, and essential services.

Building Back Better

Ukraine's reconstruction presents an opportunity to modernize infrastructure and adopt innovative building solutions. Architects and planners are working to create resilient, energy-efficient homes that meet European standards while preserving Ukrainian cultural identity and preparing for EU accession.

Canada-Ukraine Partnership

The Canada-Ukraine Chamber of Commerce is strengthening ties between Canadian organizations and Ukrainian reconstruction efforts, facilitating humanitarian aid, business cooperation, and rebuilding initiatives that support long-term recovery through trade missions and investment conferences.

Prioritizing Displaced Families

With over 3.7 million internally displaced people as of early 2025, reconstruction efforts prioritize helping families return to safe, habitable homes. New housing programs focus on affordability, accessibility, and community resilience to ensure sustainable recovery for vulnerable populations including military families.

90-120 Day Reconstruction Timeline

Modern construction methods including modular and prefabricated housing are enabling rapid reconstruction timelines. Our integrated supply chain approach delivers completed homes within 90-120 days, providing families with quick access to safe housing while maintaining high quality standards and energy efficiency.

About Us

Our Mission

Dreams Realized Charity was founded to provide comprehensive reconstruction support for Ukrainian families affected by conflict. We believe that every family deserves not just a house, but a home where dreams can be rebuilt and realized.

Our Approach

We've created a unique three-organization partnership model that provides end-to-end reconstruction support:

Dreams Realized Charity provides direct financial assistance and home essentials to families in our reconstruction program.

Dream-Building Architects & Entrepreneurs Volunteer Association offers professional architectural and design services free of charge through volunteer professionals committed to Ukraine's recovery.

Sunwell Group supplies high-quality building materials and manages construction with experienced crews, ensuring projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.

Our Values

Dignity: We treat every family with respect and ensure they have agency in the rebuilding of their homes.

Quality: We never compromise on the quality of materials, design, or construction.

Efficiency: Our integrated approach ensures projects are completed within 90-120 days.

Transparency: We maintain clear communication and accountability throughout the reconstruction process.

Community: We're not just rebuilding houses; we're helping restore communities and hope for Ukraine's future.
